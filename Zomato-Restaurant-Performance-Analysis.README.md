This project demonstrates KPI modeling, dashboard development, business performance analysis, customer behavior evaluation, and interactive visualization design for restaurant analytics.

## Business Objective

This project evaluates restaurant operational performance using Zomato restaurant data.

The analysis focuses on identifying:
- Top-performing restaurants
- Revenue concentration patterns
- Customer ordering behavior
- Cuisine demand trends
- Geographic business performance
- Relationships between ratings and revenue

The dashboard was designed to support operational monitoring and data-driven restaurant decision-making.

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains restaurant-level operational and performance data including:

- `restaurant_id` — Unique restaurant identifier  
- `restaurant_name` — Restaurant name  
- `city` — Restaurant location  
- `cuisine_type` — Cuisine category  
- `rating` — Customer rating score  
- `total_orders` — Number of orders  
- `revenue` — Restaurant revenue metrics

## Analytical Framework

### Revenue Performance Analysis

The project includes revenue-focused analysis identifying the restaurants generating the highest sales performance.

Key analytical steps:
- Ranked restaurants by total revenue
- Compared revenue concentration across top-performing restaurants
- Identified sales distribution patterns

### Order Volume Analysis

The dashboard evaluates customer demand using total order activity.

Key analytical steps:
- Measured order volume by restaurant
- Compared operational demand across restaurants
- Identified high-volume performance trends

### Ratings vs Revenue Analysis

The project analyzes the relationship between customer satisfaction and financial performance.

Key analytical steps:
- Built scatter plot relationship analysis
- Compared ratings against revenue generation
- Evaluated customer experience impact on sales

### Cuisine Performance Analysis

The dashboard evaluates revenue performance across cuisine categories.

Key analytical steps:
- Aggregated revenue by cuisine type
- Compared cuisine demand trends
- Identified highest-performing cuisine segments

### City Performance Analysis

The project analyzes restaurant performance across geographic markets.

Key analytical steps:
- Compared revenue by city
- Evaluated geographic concentration patterns
- Identified high-performing restaurant markets

## Key Findings

| Analysis Area | Findings |
|---|---|
| Revenue Performance | A small number of restaurants generated a significant share of total revenue. |
| Customer Demand | Certain restaurants consistently received substantially higher order volume. |
| Ratings Impact | Higher-rated restaurants generally demonstrated stronger revenue performance. |
| Cuisine Trends | Indian and North Indian cuisine categories generated the highest revenue. |
| Geographic Performance | Larger restaurant markets significantly outperformed smaller cities in total sales. |

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Use top-performing restaurants as benchmark operational models.
2. Improve customer satisfaction to positively influence revenue performance.
3. Expand investment into high-performing cuisine categories.
4. Optimize marketing strategies based on city-level demand trends.
5. Improve operational efficiency for high-volume restaurants during peak demand periods.
